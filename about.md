## üéØ Philosophy & Approach

**Build in Public**  
I share my learning journey including mistakes and failures. There is no such thing as a stupid question in tech.

**Automate Everything**  
If a task is repeated more than twice, it should be automated. Infrastructure as Code is essential for reliability and scalability.

**Security First**  
Security is not something added at the end. It is part of every design decision from day one.

**Document Relentlessly**  
Future you and your teammates will thank you. If it is not documented, it does not exist.

**Community Over Competition**  
The tech community thrives on sharing knowledge. I am happy to help anyone exploring DevOps or cloud engineering.
